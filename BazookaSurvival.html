Bazooka Survival

Don't take this project to serious Becouse I used Among Us sprites for fun. But I still want to put it in my protfolio becouse the project was fun to work on. I made the game using Unity and C# where you shoot enemies in a 2D world. The goal is to stay alive for 60 seconds while enemies come from both sides and try to get to you. You have a bazooka to eliminate the enemies before they get too close. But be careful because the bazooka's explosion can hurt you too. There are also exclamation marks that spawn left or right randomly. If that happens the enemy’s can only spawn on that side. You have small enemies and big enemies. You kill the small once by just hitting them. But you need to shoot the big enemies in the eyes in order to kill them. I put a lot of effort into making the game fun to play and easy to control. People liked it and found it funny when I showed it in the school arcade. Students and teachers enjoyed playing it and had to be quick and smart to survive.
