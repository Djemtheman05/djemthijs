2D platformer


This assignment was especially challenging for. In this game, you can choose who you want to play as. I used pictures of myself and classmates because I thought that was very cute and funny. The goal of the game is that you defeat the dragon on the left side. But you have to do a few steps before you get there, namely by using another dragon's fire by making a path to new enemies. Then you can kill them by jumping on them and get money for I the store healing or buy a gun, namely then you can go back to kill the dragons again. And then you have won. I enjoyed making this assignment and learned many new rules of code.
