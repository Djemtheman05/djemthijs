Catch n Fetch

I worked with DDM students to make a game using Unity and C#. First, we made a simple version of the game on paper and talked about our ideas. The DDM students used their art skills to make the game look cool. I used Unity's game-making tools to make the game work and added the art made by the DDM students. We tested and improved the game many times to make sure it was fun to play. To show off our game, I put the game on the arcade at our school. I put the game on a USB drive and connected it to the arcade machine. This lets other students and teachers play our game for themselves. This project let us mix our programming and art skills. It made a fun game that everyone at our school could enjoy and remember. The Idea of the game is simple, but I want to make multiplayer and add more mechanics to the game. You walk around and catch fruit that falls from the magic tree. Some fruits are more rare and give you more points but are also harder to catch And beehives subtracts your points. You can dash if the meter on the right is full. You also get a magic rock each 30 seconds. You can throw the rock to one of the sides and then items start to fall down more on that side.
