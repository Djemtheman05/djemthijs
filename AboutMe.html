My name is Djem Thijs.

I was born on 06-01-2005 and study software development, for example game development. I currently study at SintLucas in Eindhoven.

Another reason is because I play games in my free time and sometimes wonder how a game is put together and how they make it, These are inspirations for me to study software development.
